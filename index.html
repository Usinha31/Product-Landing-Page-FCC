About Us

Dastak foundation started as a general ngo which worked for all social causes but slowly we started working mostly for animals. We help animal shelters and animal lovers by providing appropriate legal, financial, and professional help. We also file legal cases and FIR in case of animal cruelty. We run ambulance service for injured animals and pets as well.

Our Mission

  • - To provide care and rights to animals.
  • - To create awareness towards the plight of our animals.
  • - To mobilize resources for stray adoption, ABC programs, healthcare and nutrition.
  • - To unite all animal lovers and activists for a stronger push for animal welfare, laws and rights.
  • - To raise funds for the organizations that selflessly work for animal welfare.
